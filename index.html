About Me

Born and raised in the suburbs of Cleveland I moved to the Chicago area in 2017 looking for opportunities to further my career goals and passions.

I'm a graduate of The Ohio State University, with a Bachelor's of Science in Cognititve Neuroscience and supplemental coursework in Studio Art. I have over ten years of experience in the fields of photography and graphic design.

Aside from my professional interests I am an avid motorbike rider, longtime hockey player and boxer, as well as a finisher in the Columbus marathon.
